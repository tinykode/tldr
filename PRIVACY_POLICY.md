# Privacy Policy for TL;DR - Built-in AI Summarizer

**Effective Date: December 19, 2025**

This Privacy Policy describes how the "TL;DR - Built-in AI Summarizer" Chrome extension ("the Extension") handles your information.

## 1. No Data Collection
The Extension is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do **not** collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information, browsing history, or the content of the pages you summarize.

## 2. Local Processing
All summarization is performed locally on your device using Chrome's built-in AI (Gemini Nano). Page content is processed within your browser and is never sent to any external servers or third parties.

## 3. Permissions Usage
- **activeTab**: Used only when you click the extension to read the text of the current page for summarization.
- **Host Permissions**: Allows the extension to run on the websites you choose to summarize.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The Extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Effective Date" at the top of this document.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store support page.
